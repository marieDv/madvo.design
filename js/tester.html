This project was realized within a month during my second internship at Campaigning Bureau in Vienna. The marketing agency uses a generic web-builder that allows customers to create small websites for their campaigns. During my time there, I was assigned with the task of modernizing the existing design, without losing any of its generic functionality. During the design process I got a lot of helpful feedback from my colleagues of the marketing and graphic departments, that made it easier for me to reduce my ideas to two final designs.
